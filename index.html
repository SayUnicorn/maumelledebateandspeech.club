About

The Maumelle Debate and Speech Club is a school organization based at Maumelle High School. We participate in tournaments accross the state of Arkansas. We have competed in the Little Rock Central Classic, the Mills Halloween Forensics and Debate Tournament, the North Little Rock Wildcat Classic, Student Congress, Ethics Bowl, and more!

Our Mission

Our mission is quite simple: to enable our members to become better public speakers whilst having fun. We believe that in effectively every field and occupation, public speaking skills are vital for your success. We accomplish this through the countless hours of practice that members take part in at club meetings, tournaments, and at home.
