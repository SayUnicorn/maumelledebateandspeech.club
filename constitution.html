Constituion

Article I: Name

The name of this organization shall be the Maumelle Debate and Speech Club (MDSC).

Article II: Purpose

It is the purpose of the Maumelle Debate and Speech Club to advance the public speaking skills of its members whilst having an enjoyable, yet challenging journey.

Article III: Membership

Membership is open to anyone and everyone who meet the following conditions:

  • A current student of Maumelle High School.
  • Maintain a GPA of 2.0 or higher.
  • Have keen interest in public speaking and a determination to get better.

Once a member is accepted into the MDSC, they must also abide by the follow these conditions in order to maintain membership:

  • Show up to a minimum of two meetings a month. Meetings are every Wednesday 4:00PM - 6:00PM.
  • Participate at every club meeting.
  • Go to a minimum of one tournament per semester.

Article IV: Officers

New Officers will be added through new Amendments or when the President concludes that a new position is necessary.

Section I: President

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Oversee the club to ensure that everything is running smoothly.
  • Facilitate club meetings.
  • Show up to as many club meetings as possible.

Section II: Secretary

Duties and Responsibilties:

  • To record minutes of every meeting.
  • Conduct roll call or sign in sheets
  • Ensure that the information is archived for future use (i.e. store on the website).

Section III: Chief Recruitment Officer

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Present a postive image of MDSC to the outside world.
  • Take charge of recruitment efforts.
  • Introduce prospective members to the member body at the next meeting.

Section IV: Chief Financial Officer

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Manage all finances in coordination with the club sponsor.
  • Take detailed records of all finances and calculate the
  • Ensure that the club reaches the break-even point.

Article V: Fiscal Year

The fiscal year of the MDSC shall align with the beginning and end of the Maumelle High School year.

Article VI: Defition of Debate and Speech Events

The following formats are the competition types that MSDC undertakes.

  • IPDA
  • Lincoln-Douglas
  • Policy Debate
  • Public Forum
  • Congressional Debate
  • Big Questions Debate
  • Informative Speaking
  • Origenal Oratory

Article VII: Amendments

Any member can propose an amendment. Proposed Amendments must be introduced seven days (7) prior to a voting date. In order for an amendment to pass, it must gain two thirds (2/3) majority of the total active members.
